Confused?

Keyboard

  1. Left Key - moves the claw left
  2. Right Key - moves the claw right
  3. Spacebar - drops the claw to grab an item

On-Screen Buttons

  1. Left Button - moves the claw left
  2. Right Button - moves the claw right
  3. Drop Button - drops the claw to grab an item

Mouse

Click the claw and drag it where you want. When you release the claw, it will drop and grab an item

Launch the claw and grab a toy! Use the on-screen buttons, your mouse, or your keyboard to animate the claw and try your luck.
